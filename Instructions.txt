To play the game:

1. Select a board size corresponding to the number of spaces along the board's length and width.

2. Click 'Start' to begin the countdown timer.

3. Select an initial starting position for the knight, and move the knight to legal positions (i.e. following an 'L' shape).

4. Each space can be visited only once.

5. The game ends when either the player has visited every square on the board, has run out of legal moves or the timer runs out.
